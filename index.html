Visa Services

Every Business
Visa Category

We identify the correct visa category for your situation and prepare every document to maximise first-attempt approval.

Z

Work Visa

For foreigners employed by Chinese entities. Requires work permit notification letter, employment contract, and authenticated educational qualifications.

M

Business Visit Visa

For commercial activities, trade negotiations, contract signings, or market investigation. Multiple-entry options available up to 10 years.

R

Talent Visa

Priority processing for high-end foreign experts in government-identified fields. Extended validity and simplified application requirements.

F

Exchange Visa

For cultural, scientific, or invited business exchange visits. Flexible single and multiple entry configurations with adjustable durations.
